About This Product

Our best-selling Radiance Vitamin C Serum combines 15% L-ascorbic acid with vitamin E and ferulic acid to deliver powerful brightening and antioxidant protection. This lightweight formula absorbs quickly, helping to fade dark spots, even skin tone, and boost natural radiance.

Apply 3-4 drops to clean, dry skin every morning before moisturizer and sunscreen. For best results, use consistently for at least 4 weeks.
